About Tessy-Tech foundation

🌟 About Tessy Tech Foundation

The Tessy Tech Foundation is more than just a name — it’s a movement. A vision brought to life by passionate minds who believe that technology is the bridge to a brighter future, especially for youth and underserved communities. Founded with a deep desire to educate, empower, and equip, Tessy Tech is on a mission to transform lives through digital skills and innovation.

In today’s fast-changing world, digital literacy is no longer optional — it’s essential. But for many, access to tech education is limited. That’s where Tessy Tech Foundation steps in. We work to break down the barriers to learning by offering free and low-cost programs in coding, web development, design, and digital entrepreneurship.

Our programs are tailored to be practical, inclusive, and beginner-friendly. Whether you're a child in a rural area with no prior experience or a student in the city hungry to learn how the internet works, Tessy Tech opens the door. We believe that every individual, regardless of age, gender, or background, deserves the opportunity to become a creator in the tech world — not just a consumer.

Through hands-on workshops, virtual bootcamps, mentorship programs, and community-driven projects, we help our learners build real-world projects and gain the confidence to start freelance careers, launch tech startups, or land jobs in the digital space.

The Tessy Tech Foundation also partners with schools, local tech hubs, and global organizations to scale impact, bringing innovative learning tools and inspirational role models to the forefront of education.

💡 Our Vision

To see a world where every young mind, regardless of background, has the skills, tools, and confidence to thrive in the digital age.

🚀 Our Mission

To democratize tech education, one learner at a time.

🌍 What We Offer

  • 💻 Coding tutorials (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • 🌐 Website development training
  • 🎨 Design thinking and UI/UX basics
  • 🤝 Access to mentors and project reviews
  • 🛠️ Freelance readiness and portfolio building
  • 👥 Leadership and teamwork workshops

At the heart of the Tessy Tech Foundation is a belief: Technology can change lives. But only if it’s accessible to all. We’re not just teaching code — we’re building futures.
